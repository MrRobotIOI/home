Butterfly Flocking Simulation

HLSL / Unreal Engine / Game Development

A butterfly flocking simulation that uses a boids algorithm. The boids are attracted to the player or a point of interest and avoid obstacles using distance fields. The player can also attract the boids to a specific location. A falloff is calculated by how close the boids are to each other and the point of interest giving a smoother force application.

The boids(butterflies) use a grid based system, rather than lopping through every boid we just loops through the boids in the cells of the grid close to the current boid.
